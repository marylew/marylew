////// Essential Tools (Line Essence Objects) ~ Work In Progress ~ //////////////////

The most recent phase is a concrete realization of the tool forms pictured on the cards.

A pathological or endearing attachment to the element: LINE asserts that the forms must emerge -quite uselessly- from various coagulations of yarn (concrete line).

Herein is transition from ultimately useful "tool" to flaccid, bound clot of representation, a gift of repose and devotional idol-making.
