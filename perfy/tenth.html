(10 years to the day)





At ten years and over 50 unique shows, we Bloody Noes had amassed a tremendous amount of costumes, props, slogans, songs, designs and techniques. The 10th anniversary seminar was a candid, improvised behind-the-scenes show-and-tell, showcasing our old favorites (you might have missed) while setting up & prepping for a 'typical' bloody noes show that finished the evening.


items pictured: Gas Can Scam the Flowchart -- Dada For Business placard -- our trusty planning whiteboard -- antique foot vibrator (?) that walks around (in front of vox amp) --



items pictured: The Eye Star, a mutable symbol from American Dream -- beloved Fog machine (courtesy Ted F) -- The Flat Earth rotating wheel from Measures -- a staple bottle of Blood (on stage) dating from Mem -- the fresh logo made for the event (see above)-- the lovely water bottle that I lost during a car break-in later that year :(
